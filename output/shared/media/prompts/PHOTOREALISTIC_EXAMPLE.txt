PHOTOREALISTIC IMAGE PROMPT EXAMPLE
====================================

FORMULA:
[Subject] + [Photography Style] + [Camera/Lens] + [Lighting] + [Color Grade]
+ Negative prompt: [Anti-AI-tells]


EXAMPLE PROMPT:
---------------
Professional woman working on MacBook Pro in bright modern co-working space, 
candid focused expression, shot on Canon EOS R5 85mm f/1.4, 
natural window light from left side, shallow depth of field with soft bokeh, 
authentic workspace environment, editorial lifestyle photography,
subtle Lightroom color grade, genuine moment captured

Negative prompt: stock photo, posed, fake, plastic skin, oversaturated, 
HDR, illustration, CGI, perfect symmetry, cartoon, artificial, waxy,
3d render, anime, uncanny valley, airbrushed, bad anatomy, 
distorted features, extra limbs, malformed hands


ESSENTIAL ELEMENTS:
-------------------
1. CAMERA: "shot on Canon EOS R5" (or Sony A7R IV, Hasselblad, etc.)
2. LENS: "85mm f/1.4" (defines depth of field and perspective)
3. LIGHTING: "natural window light" or "studio softbox lighting"
4. STYLE: "editorial/commercial/lifestyle photography"
5. COLOR: "subtle Lightroom color grade" or "VSCO preset style"
6. NEGATIVE: Always include the anti-AI-tells list above


CAMERA/LENS QUICK REFERENCE:
----------------------------
Portraits:     Canon EOS R5, 85mm f/1.4
Products:      Hasselblad H6D, 120mm macro
Interiors:     Sony A7R IV, 24mm tilt-shift
Wide scenes:   Canon 5D Mark IV, 35mm f/1.4


LIGHTING QUICK REFERENCE:
-------------------------
Natural:       "golden hour sunlight", "soft diffused daylight", "window light"
Studio:        "three-point lighting", "softbox", "beauty dish"
Dramatic:      "Rembrandt lighting", "chiaroscuro", "single source"
Commercial:    "high-key studio", "even illumination", "no harsh shadows"
